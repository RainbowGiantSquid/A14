Primary & Secondary English and Maths

The Literacy and Numeracy programme runs from 10am – 1pm for each age group and includes two hours of literacy support and one hour of numeracy support. Using our accredited testing system, our fully qualified teachers are able to determine each pupil’s strong and weak areas of learning. Using our excellent resources and inspiring teaching, this programme then strengthens each pupil’s weakest areas allowing them to progress more quickly.

GCSE Courses

We offer structured GCSE revision courses for Year 10 & 11 pupils preparing for exams in English, Maths, and Science. We work to all exam board specifications and provide courses which give pupils a structure for preparing for these important exams. Courses run on Saturdays from 10 – 1 and all classes are five pupils maximum to ensure pupils get time to work on specific problems and make real progress.

Evening Classes

Some of our branches run Literacy & Numeracy, 11+, and GCSE courses on weekday evenings from 6pm – 9pm. If this is of interest, please do get in touch and we will be able to give you more information.
